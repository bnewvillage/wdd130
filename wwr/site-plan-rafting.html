Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the website is to showcase our water rafting services. To serve as a portal for people to find us easier. Current prices and packages, and ongoing promotional deals. It will also highlight our upcoming events and our recent successful events.

Audience

Our target customers are primarily adults ages 19 and above, they are in financially stable homes, they thrill-seekers and adventure-lovers. They want to take on challenges in a pseudo-nature setting and working with a team. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They will expect to find photos and videos of our routes, our prices, our schedule, our location, as well as other services we offer and what we don't offer. What are their needs that aren't being met? They need a quick and easy way to view our services and get in contact with us, to make their decision-making as easy as possible. How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? My audience will likely usually access my site through their laptops. For easier navigation and larger screens to scan our media.
